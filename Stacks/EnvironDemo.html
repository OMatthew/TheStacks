How to play?
1) The game may take a while to load. Click “Play” to begin.
2) Choose the scenario that you’d like to play. The 'Earth' scenario is most applicable to The Stacks.
3) Select the persona that you want to play as. Each one gives a unique gameplay advantage.
4) Click “Walkthrough” for a detailed step-by-step guide about how to play.
5) If you'd like to skip the Walkthrough click "I’ve played before" and zoom out or click the Earth button to access the sector menu options.
6) Click a sector to access, research and choose investments. Return to the globe to observe the consequences of your investments and to deal with events that happen around the globe.
7) Continue researching and investing as you try to create a sustainable world (i.e. maximize the Environment and Economy scores, which are displayed at the bottom right in the minimap).
