The Stacks are an installation that engages library visitors in reflection and conversation about their neighborhoods and their city.

We ask you to consider the places you know and to share your thoughts, experiences, concerns, and questions.

Mark The Map

Share your favorite places in Austin. Tell others about the places that need to be better. What would you do about them? Get to know what others think about Austin - all in one map!
Whiteboard

A digital whiteboard at your fingertips! Use the whiteboard to explain your idea, express yourself, or organize your thoughts.
Environ

Learn about energy sources and technologies, and their effect on the environment and the economy. Play Environ and try to balance the cost and environmental consequences of your energy policy decisions.
Geoguessr

Travel around the world, randomly, without leaving the library. Get to know random spaces around the world and try to guess where they are located.
