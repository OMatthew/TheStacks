Instructions
The Speakup Austin platform is a tool for citizens in Austin to engage with the City about the changes and recommendations they have to improve the City of Austin.
1) You can browse through the existing Ideas by scrolling down the window and tapping on the specific idea.
2) You can filter the ideas by tapping on the filters underneath the "Submit" button: by latest idea, most popular, ideas in action, or selecting a specific filter.
3) The "Enter your idea here..." entry box will prompt ideas related to your submission.
4) You can create your own Idea. Tap on Submit and fill in the details the for your entry. Unfortunately, you won't be able to attach images by using The Stacks; we encourage you to create your own profile and submit a complete entry using your personal devices or other library computers.
