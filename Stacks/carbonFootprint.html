How to play?
Your Carbon Footprint is the amount of Carbon Dioxide emissions associated with your particular day-to-day decisions and way of living. It provides a reference to understand the impact you have on the environment in terms of CO2 one of the main contributors to global pollution.Estimate your footprint by answering a few questions and learn what you can do to reduce your impact.
1) Select the location where you want your carbon footprint to be estimated by tapping on one of the orange icons in the map.
2) Tap "Begin" and "Get Started". Do not try to login.
3) You can randomly generate your character or take your time to personalize yours.
4) Answer the prompts you are given. You can provide basic information or detailed information depending on how confident you feel about the details you are being asked. Drag the meters right or left for each option.
5) Track your progress on the bar to the lower right of the screen. Answering all the questions is necessary in order to calculate your carbon footprint.
6) Once you are done reading your results, you can select to edit your answers or explore scenarios to reduce your footprint.
7) If you are done, do not tap "Save Your Footprint". Return to The Stacks homepage by tapping on the return arrow button in the lower left side of the screen.
